Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026

Fiduciary Experts LLC ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use the 401(k) Enrollment Guide and related tools hosted on this website.

Information We Collect

When you use the Enrollment Guide, we may collect the following information:

  • Personal Information: First name, last name, date of birth, email address, and phone number
  • Financial Information: Gross and net paycheck amounts, current 401(k) balance, contribution percentages, and retirement age
  • Assessment Data: Risk tolerance questionnaire responses and investment preferences
  • Meeting Records: Date of visit, advisor name, meeting reason, and notes (collected by advisors during 1-on-1 sessions)

How We Use Your Information

  • Enrollment Guidance: To provide personalized retirement planning projections, risk assessments, and investment education
  • Email Summaries: To send you a summary of your enrollment guide selections via email (if you provide an email address)
  • Advisor Tracking: To maintain records of participant meetings for plan administration and compliance purposes
  • Reporting: To generate aggregate reports on enrollment activity for plan sponsors

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate this tool:

  • Smartsheet: Participant meeting records are stored in Smartsheet for tracking and reporting. Smartsheet's privacy policy applies to data stored on their platform.
  • EmailJS: Email summaries are sent through EmailJS. EmailJS processes your email address to deliver the message. No email content is stored by EmailJS beyond delivery.
  • Vercel: This website is hosted on Vercel. Vercel may collect standard web server logs (IP address, browser type, pages visited).
  • Fidelity Investments: When you click "Submit and Go to Fidelity," you are redirected to Fidelity NetBenefits, which is governed by Fidelity's own privacy policy. We do not share your data with Fidelity through this tool.

Data Storage and Retention

  • Browser Storage: Some enrollment data is temporarily stored in your browser's local storage to allow cross-tab functionality. This data is not transmitted to any server and is cleared when you clear your browser data.
  • Smartsheet Records: Participant meeting records are retained in Smartsheet for the duration required by the plan sponsor and applicable regulations.
  • No Permanent Server Storage: This tool does not maintain its own database. Calculations and projections are performed in your browser and are not stored on our servers.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, including:

  • API tokens and credentials are stored as server-side environment variables, not in client-facing code
  • Admin portal access is password-protected with session timeout
  • Rate limiting is applied to prevent API abuse
  • All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption

Your Rights

  • You may choose not to provide personal information; however, some features (such as email summaries) require an email address
  • You may request deletion of your meeting records by contacting your plan administrator or Fiduciary Experts
  • You may clear browser-stored data at any time through your browser settings

Children's Privacy

This tool is not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
