Actual take-home pay will vary based on your individual tax situation, benefits elections, and other payroll deductions. Pretax contributions are deducted before federal and state income taxes, reducing your current taxable income; Roth contributions are made with after-tax dollars and do not reduce your current taxable income, but qualified withdrawals in retirement are tax-free.
